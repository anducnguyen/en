About Me

Hi, I am Andrew

Welcome to my page!

I'm a Master student in Mobility Systems Group, Suzlab at Nagoya University, Japan, working with Professor T. Suzuki. My current research is in the area of systems identification and modeling, plus dynamics control with applications focusing for connected and automated vehicles. I’m also working with Dr. Hatem at ZaTitech®, an autonomous technology start-up based in Nagoya city. Just so you know, Nagoya is the biggest city in Aichi Prefecture, a.k.a the central hub of products exporting and automotive technology of Japan. It's been known for being the hometown of Toyota, Denso, and a lot more automotive companies!

Personally, I am keen on lots of topics, including fundamental science (just physics and math), technology, sports (basketball - hand, soccer - foot, swim - whole body, running - cardio), society, well and a little bit of philosophy (both Western and Eastern). And as you can guess, I am also a Car lover! I will be excited if you also find some common interests.

More of my info can be found at my Linkedin. I would be very happy if we can get in touch and share ideas.

Cheers!
